Empty states should explain what happened and what to do next.

## Example

<div class="demo" style="align-items: stretch;">
  <div class="of-empty">
    <div class="of-empty__title">No engagements yet</div>
    <div class="of-empty__body">Start your first engagement to track tasks, notes, and outcomes in one place.</div>
    <button class="of-btn of-btn--primary of-btn--md">Create engagement</button>
  </div>
</div>

## Guidelines

- Keep copy specific and reassuring.
- Always provide a clear next action.
- Avoid dead-end empty states.
