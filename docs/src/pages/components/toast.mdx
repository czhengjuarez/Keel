Use toasts for non-blocking feedback after save, publish, or sync actions.

## Example

<div class="demo demo__stack" style="align-items: stretch; max-width: 420px;">
  <div class="of-toast">
    <div class="of-toast__title">Saved changes.</div>
    <div class="of-toast__body">Your engagement summary is now up to date.</div>
  </div>
  <div class="of-toast of-toast--success">
    <div class="of-toast__title">Invite sent.</div>
    <div class="of-toast__body">A confirmation email is on the way.</div>
  </div>
</div>

## Accessibility

- Announce toast content in a polite live region.
- Keep text short and actionable.
- Toasts should not trap keyboard focus.
