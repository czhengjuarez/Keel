Use tooltips for brief helper text, not critical instructions.

## Example

<div class="demo demo__row of-tooltip-demo" style="min-height: 120px; align-items: flex-start;">
  <button class="of-btn of-btn--secondary of-btn--md of-tooltip-demo__trigger" aria-describedby="tooltip-refresh-hint">Hover me</button>
  <span id="tooltip-refresh-hint" role="tooltip" class="of-tooltip of-tooltip-demo__bubble">Data refreshes every 15 minutes.</span>
</div>

## Accessibility

- Tooltip content should be available on hover and focus.
- Keep text short.
- Do not put interactive controls inside tooltips.
