Use tooltips for brief helper text, not critical instructions.

## Example

<div class="demo demo__row" style="position: relative; min-height: 120px; align-items: flex-start;">
  <button class="of-btn of-btn--secondary of-btn--md">Hover me</button>
  <span class="of-tooltip">Data refreshes every 15 minutes.</span>
</div>

## Accessibility

- Tooltip content should be available on hover and focus.
- Keep text short.
- Do not put interactive controls inside tooltips.
